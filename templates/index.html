About Rentify

Rentify is a fashion rental platform that lets you rent premium outfits without buying them. Whether it’s a wedding, party, or special occasion — wear designer clothes without long-term commitment.

Unlike traditional delivery platforms, Rentify follows a pickup-based model. You simply choose an outfit online and collect it directly from the seller’s store or wardrobe.
